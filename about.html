About Us

Located in the heart of Sapele Delta State, Rebar is an exquisite restaurant that offers a unique culinary experience to its customers. With a focus on sustainable and locally sourced ingredients, our menu is constantly changing and evolving. The ambiance of our restaurant is warm, inviting, and stylishly modern, creating the perfect setting for casual dining with friends or for intimate dates. Our drink selection also extends beyond wine to include craft cocktails, beers, and a variety of non-alcoholic beverages.
